About Me

As a passionate software engineer, my journey has been shaped by a solid educational foundation. With a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering from Mekelle University, Ethiopia, I acquired a comprehensive understanding of software modelling, development, testing, and more. In parallel, I pursued a Bachelor of Arts in Management at Unity University, Ethiopia, enhancing my skills in project management, business strategy, and leadership.

Building upon these academic accomplishments, I am currently pursuing an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's in Green Networking and Cloud Computing, spanning multiple disciplines across the University of Lorraine, France, Leeds Beckett University, UK, and Lulea University of Technology, Sweden. This multidimensional program equips me with expertise in complex systems engineering, sustainable computing, and advanced technological concepts(Cloud services) , fostering a holistic understanding of the tech landscape.

With real-world experience as a Full Stack Developer at Sabawyan Tech in Mekelle, Ethiopia, I collaborated on designing, developing, and maintaining web applications. This role Refined my skills in meeting client requirements while ensuring application functionality, security, and performance.

I'm very interested in working in DevOps and Cloud Computing. My aspiration extends to working in these fields in Africa, recognizing the untapped potential in this region. Coming from a developing country with different community problems, I strongly believe in technology's capacity to solve these problems. In the future, I aim to contribute to overcoming these challenges and give back to my community. Additionally, I firmly advocate for diversity in tech; I envision supporting young girls in achieving their tech dreams, understanding the significance of their inclusion in this space.
